Books

साँझ- सवेरे black & white

When nature talks to you, it has no words but when you talk to nature, you need words and more words.

Welcome to a journey of words, where each verse is a step into the landscape of the soul. This collection, साँझ- सवेरे black & white, is a mosaic of moments—woven together with threads of emotion, reflection, and insight. Each poetic verse within these pages is a whisper from the heart, an echo of personal experiences and universal truths.

As you turn these pages, I invite you to find solace in the familiar, wonder in the unknown, and perhaps a piece of yourself in the verses that follow. Thank you for embarking on this poetic journey with me. I hope you find as much meaning in these poems as I found in writing them.

साँझ- सवेरे black & white – Part 2

This is not just a collection of poems but pieces of my inner journey. They are not written in a rush, but like how a night sky is filled with myriad stars slowly and gradually.

Following the footsteps of my first book (also available on Amazon), this new volume साँझ- सवेरे black & white part-2 continues to explore life philosophy through verses. Originally written in Hindi and then translated into English—I hope the language won't matter, but every poem is expected to meet you where you are and gently lead you inward.

I welcome you all to the pages of my heart... of my life.
